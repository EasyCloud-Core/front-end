About Us

Cloud experts committed to simplicity

Our Story

Founded in 2015, EasyCloud began as a small team of cloud architects frustrated by how complicated cloud hosting had become. We set out to build tools and services that would make cloud computing accessible to everyone, not just large enterprises with dedicated IT teams.

Today, we've helped over 1,200 businesses successfully migrate to and manage their cloud infrastructure across AWS, Google Cloud, and Microsoft Azure.

Our Values

  • Simplicity: We believe technology should make life easier, not more complicated.
  • Transparency: No hidden fees or confusing pricing structures.
  • Expertise: Our team includes certified cloud professionals from all major platforms.
